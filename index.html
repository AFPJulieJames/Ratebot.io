Why the rates hold up

Numbers a customer can't argue with.

RateBot isn't a guess. Every quote is grounded in real fuel data and validated two ways.

Nationwide regional diesel data

Prices come from the U.S. Energy Information Administration, broken out by region. Lanes anywhere in the country are priced on their own region's diesel (East Coast, Midwest, Gulf, Rocky Mountain, and the West Coast including California), not averaged into one national number.

Real load math

Multi-vehicle orders are counted by VIN, and the fuel increase is shared across the carrier's full load, the way pricing actually works on the road.

Validated two ways

Quotes are checked against live broker market rates and against each company's own historical prices, so they survive a customer's pushback.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is RateBot?

RateBot is a re-quoting tool for auto transport brokers. It takes your past order history and re-prices every lane you've shipped at today's regional diesel rates, then shows you which past customers to call and what to quote them.

How does RateBot price a lane?

Each lane is matched to the diesel price of its EIA region on the day you shipped it and again today. The fuel change is shared across the carrier's full load and applied to your historical cost, then your target margin produces a customer-ready quote.

Where does the diesel data come from?

Official U.S. Energy Information Administration on-highway diesel prices, broken out by region. Lanes nationwide are priced on their own region's diesel (East Coast, Midwest, Gulf, Rocky Mountain, and the West Coast including California) rather than averaged into one national number.

Do you cover the whole country?

Yes. RateBot covers lanes nationwide. Every lane is priced on the diesel of its own EIA region, so coast-to-coast and everything in between is handled accurately, not with a single national average.

Is my customer data private?

Yes. Your tool is built from your own order export and is private to you. Your data is never shared with other companies.

Do I need to install anything?

No. RateBot is a web tool you open on any device. There is nothing to install and no app to download.

What do I need to send to get started?

Just the order export your transport management system already produces, with lanes, dates, costs and vehicle counts. We handle the cleanup and the rest.
